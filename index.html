My Five turn ONs when looking for a partner?

There are many qualities that can make someone attractive, but here are a few to consider:

  • Kindness and compassion
  • Intelligence and humor
  • Shared values and interests
  • Communication and honesty
  • Supportiveness and encouragement

My Five turn OFFs when looking for a partner?

Just like turn ons, there are certain qualities that can be dealbreakers. Here are a few to consider:

  • Dishonesty or deception
  • Disrespect or inconsideration
  • Unreliability or flakiness
  • Negativity or pessimism
  • Emotional unavailability
